Supported Systems

NES runs on jsNES. GBA runs on EmulatorJS (mGBA core) — loaded on demand from CDN the first time you pick a GBA ROM. For fullscreen, use your browser's built-in fullscreen, or for GBA tap the ⛶ icon inside the EmulatorJS menu.

Controls
NES

Keyboard: Arrows/WASD, Z/J/N (A), X/K/M (B), Enter/Space (Start), Shift/C (Select). On phones, use the on-screen pad below the game.

GBA

EmulatorJS handles its own input — tap the gamepad icon in the EmulatorJS menu bar for touch controls, or configure key mappings from its gear icon.
